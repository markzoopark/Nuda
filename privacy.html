Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nuda ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our browser extension.

Information Collection

Nuda does not collect any personal data. The only information stored is your font preferences, which are saved locally on your device using browser storage.

We do not:

  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect personal information
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Permissions

Our extension requires permissions to:

  • Access websites to apply font changes
  • Use storage to save your preferences

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension and not for data collection.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's privacy policy page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page in the Chrome Web Store.
